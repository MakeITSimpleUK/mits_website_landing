ABOUT US

At Make IT Simple, web applications make us giddy.

In fact, it’s pretty much all we do!

We help create and develop web applications that use web browsers and technology to enable you to complete tasks and activities on the internet. For example, we have developed health and safety management systems, umbrella payroll software and middle and back office recruitment systems.

These days there isn’t much you can’t do in a browser that you can on desktop. With funky technology like electron, often companies deploy web applications to the desktop, rather than building natively for desktop.
